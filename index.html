Placing Literature

Placing Literature is a crowd-sourced database of real-world locations from literature. It is an attempt to examine the connection between real-world places and the stories they inspire. The data is presented on a map which can be explored or updated. Check it out: Placing Literature

sentimental

Yet another micro-blogging sentiment analyzer. How to measure sentiment? Started with a very simple hypothesis: Count 'nice' words and 'mean' words in a tweet and score the tweet. Mix in features unrelated to content - location, user activity, followers and friends. Stir. Estimate.
